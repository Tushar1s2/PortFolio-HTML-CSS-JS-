About me

Hey there! I’m Tushar Singh Panwar, an enthusiastic learner and developer currently pursuing my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at DIT University, Dehradun (2024–2028 batch). I’ve always been fascinated by how technology shapes the world around us, and that curiosity motivates me to explore, learn, and build creative digital solutions every day.

I’m in my learning journey right now, building a solid foundation in both web development and problem-solving. I’ve completed the frontend part using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, and have just started exploring Node.js for backend development. On the programming side, I’m strengthening my Java DSA skills and currently working through Dynamic Programming (DP) to improve my logic-building abilities.

My goal is to become a skilled Full Stack Developer, mastering the MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) and developing impactful, real-world applications. I focus on writing clean, optimized code and designing user-friendly interfaces that combine creativity with functionality — a balance that defines a good developer in my eyes.

Beyond coding, I love laptop gaming and keeping up with the latest tech reviews — whether it’s newgraphics cards, laptops, mobiles, TVs, or even smart fridges. Exploring new technology across every domain excites me and keeps me inspired to innovate and grow with the ever-evolving digital world.
